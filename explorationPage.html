Kathmandu

Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal, is a vibrant cultural hub rich in history and tradition. Known for its ancient temples, bustling streets, and breathtaking views of the Himalayas, Kathmandu offers a blend of spirituality and adventure. Visit iconic landmarks like Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple), Pashupatinath, and Kathmandu Durbar Square to witness the city's deep-rooted heritage.

Pokhara

Pokhara is a paradise for travelers, offering stunning lakes, breathtaking mountain views, and thrilling adventure activities. Located in central Nepal, Pokhara is home to the serene Phewa Lake, the mystical caves of Mahendra and Gupteshwor, and the exhilarating Sarangkot sunrise viewpoint. Whether you love trekking, paragliding, or just soaking in nature, Pokhara is the perfect destination.

Pokhara

Phewa Lake

Phewa Lake is the second-largest lake in Nepal and a major attraction in Pokhara. The lake offers stunning reflections of the Annapurna mountains, making it a perfect spot for boating, fishing, and photography. The famous Tal Barahi Temple sits on an island in the middle of the lake, adding to its beauty and spiritual significance.

Cost: $70

Sarangkot

Sarangkot is a popular viewpoint known for its breathtaking sunrise views over the Annapurna range. It is also a famous destination for paragliding, offering an unforgettable aerial experience above Pokhara Valley. Many travelers visit Sarangkot for trekking, adventure sports, and stunning panoramic landscapes.

Cost: $90

Davis Falls

Davis Falls, also known as "Patale Chhango," is a mesmerizing waterfall in Pokhara. The water from Phewa Lake rushes underground through a series of caves, creating a stunning natural phenomenon. Visitors can explore the nearby Gupteshwor Cave and enjoy the unique beauty of this hidden waterfall. It is a must-visit spot for those seeking both natural wonders and cultural experiences.

Cost: $80
